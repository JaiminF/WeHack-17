What is Wehack?

Wehack is VIT University's annual hackathon event, bringing together most creative and passionate students from universities all over the world for 36 hours of hacking to create ground-breaking technologies from scratch in a weekend.

Participants will be trading their sleep for a weekend and create diverse teams to compete to build, test and launch their solutions for pressing challenges, together in an environment optimized for creativity.

Past Hackathons

We love Hackathons

#WeHack 2014 was India’s first Wearable Technology Hackathon. Tackling challenges in the sectors of Consumer, Energy Conservation, Gaming and Healthcare, it garnered a crowd of over 150 participants and a myriad of innovative ideas in its maiden hackathon.

Our next tryst with innovation was Makeathon 2014, which saw a participation of over 450 competitors, with Automation and Aerospace, Healthcare, Media Sciences and Accessibility, Energy Conservation as its challenging tracks.

This was followed by a Hackathon in 2014.This was our biggest hackathon by far and brought in excess of 1300 participants.

#WeHack 2015 took all the amazing elements that made the first iteration amazing and greatly improved on it, where the ingenuity was set as a basic parameter for the participants.

Attracting a crowd of over 500 participants, it was a huge success in terms of the number of projects and their creativity. Projects covered a vast range of challenges, implementing IoT – Internet of Things – the theme of that hackathon.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for WeHack?
Wehack is for undergraduates and MBA students from any college in the world. Unfortunately, high school and graduate students can’t attend.
What about teams?
If you don’t have a team now, don’t stress. We will have team formation and pitching session geared towards helping you find people to work with.
Will there be food and accomodation?
Yes, We will provide catered meals during the event along with free caffeine! We will also provide accomodation inside the campus for the participants.
Will certificates be provided?
Yes, recognized certificates will be provided to all the participants.
What do I need to bring?
Please bring a student ID so we can verify and register you on-site. You will also need to bring a laptop, whatever software you feel might be necessary for development. Teammates are free to share equipment with each other.
